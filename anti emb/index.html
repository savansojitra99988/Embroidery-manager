Firebase Cloud Sync Setup

Follow these simple steps to sync your embroidery business data across all devices automatically:

  1. Go to the Firebase Console and log in with your Google account.
  2. Click Add Project and name it something like Embroidery Manager. Follow the prompts (you can disable Google Analytics).
  3. In your project dashboard, click the Web icon (</>) to register a new web app. Name it embroidery-pwa.
  4. Copy the firebaseConfig credentials shown on the screen (the values for apiKey, appId, databaseURL, etc.).
  5. In your left sidebar menu, expand Build and click Realtime Database.
  6. Click Create Database, select your region, and choose Start in Test Mode (so the app can read/write data). Click Enable.
  7. Open the file firebase-config.js in your project directory and replace the placeholders with your copied credentials.
Note: Once configured, your existing local data will automatically upload to your secure cloud database.
